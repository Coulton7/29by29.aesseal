£29 million by 2029 investment pledge

29by29 Investment Summary

Learn about our environmental investment pledge

Our ‘£29 million by 2029’ pledge recognises that businesses will have a potentially huge influence on the success of efforts to limit global warming.

By committing to take the global company beyond Net Zero globally (we have already gone beyond Net Zero in the UK business) and explaining how we are going to do it, we believe we will provide a road map for other companies and organisations who share our concern about ethical, balanced and sustainable development.

29 by 29 Investment Summary

The AES Engineering Ltd. Group has committed to invest £29 million on environmental projects between January 1, 2021 and April 1, 2029. Part of the commitment was an undertaking to report on progress, so we are doing what we said we would.
